Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani

Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani is a delicious and aromatic rice dish made with marinated chicken pieces layered with fragrant basmati rice, caramelized onions, and flavorful spices.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
  • 500 grams chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 4 tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 green chilies, slit
  • 1/4 cup mint leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon biryani masala
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, biryani masala, salt, and half of the chopped mint and coriander leaves. Let it marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan and fry the sliced onions until golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add the whole spices and saut√© for a minute. Then add the marinated chicken pieces and cook until they are partially cooked.
  4. Layer the partially cooked chicken with soaked basmati rice, fried onions, chopped tomatoes, green chilies, and the remaining mint and coriander leaves. Repeat the layers if needed.
  5. Cover the pan with a tight lid and cook on low heat for about 20-25 minutes or until the rice is fully cooked and the chicken is tender.
  6. Once done, gently fluff up the biryani with a fork and serve hot with raita or salan.
