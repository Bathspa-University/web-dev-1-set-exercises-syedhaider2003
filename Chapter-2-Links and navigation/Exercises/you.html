YOU

Plot

"You" is a psychological thriller television series that follows the story of Joe Goldberg, a charming yet disturbed bookstore manager, portrayed by Penn Badgley. Joe becomes infatuated with the people he meets, particularly women, and uses social media and other means to gather information about them. He soon becomes obsessed with a woman named Guinevere Beck, played by Elizabeth Lail, and goes to extreme lengths to insert himself into her life. The series explores themes of love, obsession, and the dark side of human nature.

Key Information

  • Genre: Psychological thriller
  • Release Year: 2018
  • Creator: Greg Berlanti, Sera Gamble
  • Awards: Nominated for several awards, including the People's Choice Award for Favorite Drama TV Show

Characters

Joe Goldberg: A bookstore manager who becomes infatuated with the people he meets, particularly women.

Guinevere Beck: An aspiring writer who becomes the object of Joe's obsession.

Love Quinn: Joe's new love interest in the second season, who has her own secrets.

Peach Salinger: Beck's wealthy and controlling best friend.

Forty Quinn: Love's twin brother, struggling with addiction and identity.

Candace Stone: Joe's ex-girlfriend who resurfaces from his past.

Dr. Nicky: A therapist who becomes entangled in Joe's web of lies.
